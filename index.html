My Idea

I've been thinking about a new way for consumers to access food on the go through completely automated food carts. Food and drinks like boba tea, tacos, and burrito bowls are well within the realm of being made by machines and with these carts, food would be readily available to people walking on the streets by tracking data on foot traffic and having the food cart station at the optimal space.
